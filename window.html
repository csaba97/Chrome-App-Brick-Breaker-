Brick Breaker game


  • Press the Left mouse button to fire the ball
  • Move the paddle to left or right with the mouse
  • Use P to pause the game
  • Your progress will be saved during gameplays
    but you can play your favourite level choosing it
    from this dropdown menu
  • Every type of brick can be broken except the grey one(it is unbreakable)

If you want to continue where you left, press the START button
